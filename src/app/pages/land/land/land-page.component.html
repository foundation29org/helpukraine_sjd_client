BANER CABECERA IMAGEN + OBJETIVO HUB

This application connects patients with a rare disease from Ukraine with organizations and hospitals, members of the European Reference Network (ERN), with the aim to provide the specialized medical support they may need during this emergency.

Make a request

Contact us if:

You are a patient

  • You have been diagnosed with a rare disease, you are in need of a specialized medical support or have an unmet acute clinical need at the moment.
  • You have been permanent resident of Ukraine and you are currently affected by the war.

You are a patient

  • Working in a hospital, NGO, patient organization or emergency response entity in contact with a patient who has been diagnosed with a rare disease or high suspicion of having a rare disease.
Make a request

{{'landraito.t2' | translate }}

1. Contact us

Create your account to identify yourself as patient or physician. You can also make a phone call or send an email.

2. Explain your medical need

Provide your basic information: what organization should help you, where you are and what you may need.

3. Get contacted

The Hub team will process the request together with the ERN and will get in touch to try to give you the aid you may need.
